About Our Collection

Criteria / Requirements / Standards / Conditions

In order to join our esteemed gallery, one must be...

...a favourite.
We personally must hold some fondness for the person in question, or their works.
...famous.
No riffraff! One must at the very least have a dedicated wikipedia page.
...an author.
The candidate in question must be famous for their works of prose or poetry. Screenplays and radio dramas can scoot.
...not Charles Dickens
Enough said.

We recognize classics and contemporaries both. After all, it is said that:

Iram indeed is gone with all his Rose,
And Jamshyd's Sev'n-ring'd Cup where no one knows;
But still a Ruby kindles in the Vine,
And many a Garden by the Water blows.
